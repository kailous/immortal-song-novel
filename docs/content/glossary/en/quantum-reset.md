# Quantum Reset and Spacetime Mutation
> Core Mechanism · Hard-SF Foundational Logic

## I. The Unit 731 “Super-Reaction Furnace” Incident
**Background**: In August 1945, before retreating, Unit 731 threw test subjects, top-secret files, rare-metal catalysts, and concentrated biochemical agents into a giant cremation furnace.

**Atomic-level doping**: The sealed high-temperature, high-pressure chamber became a natural reaction vessel. Melted platinum, tungsten, and other rare metals mixed with vaporized compounds and the skeletal frame in an extremely rare atomic-doping event. The mutation was engraved into the **bone lattice**, not the blood or muscle.

**Quantum save-state**: The information density of the burning secret files, combined with the extreme energy concentration, forced a closed loop onto the protagonist’s telomeres. Every time he suffers fatal destruction, the skeletal trait triggers a localized quantum reversal and restores the body to the “perfect initial state” completed in 1945.

## II. How Quantum Reset Operates
**Not creation from nothing**: Reconstruction still obeys conservation laws. The skeleton draws thermal energy, electromagnetic energy, and even raw material from surrounding organic matter to rebuild flesh.

**Cost (post-reconstruction weakness)**: A full rebuild causes overwhelming hunger and a temporary state of severe physical weakness.

**Neuro-topology lag**: Muscles and bones reassemble quickly, but consciousness depends on a high-dimensional topology of billions of neurons and trillions of synapses. Quantum reset hits a severe **data bottleneck** here. The body returns almost instantly, but full restoration of awareness can take months or longer. This explains the protagonist’s amnesia after catastrophic brain damage and the gaps in the bracelet’s unlock sequence.

## III. Absolute Deadlock: The Gene Cannot Be Inherited
This mutation is a **singular anomaly** produced at one specific spacetime point in 1945 under one specific chemical mix and energy density. It is deadlocked into the deepest layer of the protagonist’s genome alone.

His descendants may inherit an excellent genetic baseline—longevity and exceptional immunity—but they can never inherit “quantum reset,” a trait that effectively violates the second law of thermodynamics.

That mechanism fixes the protagonist’s fate: he must personally outlive every loved one and every child. It establishes the tragic core of the **civilization watcher** and prevents an immortal bloodline from crushing ordinary civilization by sheer dimensional advantage.

## IV. Expert Commentary
- **Physicist**: At its core, this forcibly entangles an individual’s physical state with a stable stream of information, granting limited privilege against macroscopic entropy.
- **Neuroscientist**: Asynchronous neural recovery is a hardware safeguard and an upper biological limit when high-dimensional data is reloaded.
- **Setting Director**: The mechanism removes the halo of godhood and recasts the protagonist as a cursed sentinel. That tragic inversion is central to the project’s anti-cliché design.
