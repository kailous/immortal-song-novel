# Watcher “Ne-Zha”
> Secret Imperial Investigator · Parallel-Cognition Mutant

## Core Role
- **Codename**: Ne-Zha, a reference to “three heads and six arms,” symbolizing high-parallel mental processing.
- **Position**: Top-tier secret investigator of the Song Federation Inspectorate and honorary guardian of the imperial house.
- **Background**: Born late in the great unification wars and raised under the protagonist’s harsh martial household code, he understands the limits of imperial power.
- **Public image**: Externally he appears precociously calm and intelligent, the sacred child of the empire in the eyes of millions.

## Ability: Parallel Cognition
**Biological basis**: An unusually developed corpus callosum allows information to pass between hemispheres at roughly ten times the normal rate, with semi-independent conscious processing on both sides.

**What it looks like in practice**:
- **Dual observation**: When facing a suspect, one side of the brain analyzes statements and financial traces while the other side reads microexpressions and emotional fluctuation.
- **Parallel inference / predictive analysis**: While processing immense administrative data flows, he can identify the “systemic cancers” hidden deep inside the bureaucracy—corruption nodes operating just outside formal rules and already penetrated by interest groups.

**Cost**: His brain is under permanent heavy load, requiring long sleep and making him somewhat isolated. Ordinary thought appears slow and transparent to him.

## Main Drive: Purge the Rot, Protect the Family Name
He spends most of his life unseen, physically removing compradors and conspirators who try to exploit reverence for imperial authority as a ladder to power.

In rare cases, he uses his investigative mandate to intervene before an execution order is signed when an elder brother is wavering on the brink.

His one wish is to keep the system so transparent that his parents never again have to face the tragedy of “gods killing gods.”

## Signature Line
“Father kills you because Song law demands it. I pull you back because you are my brother. If you still sign that contract, then before these eyes close, I can only cry for Father one more time.”
