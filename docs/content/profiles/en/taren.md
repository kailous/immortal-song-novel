# The Taren

![The Taren](../../../images/塔伦人.webp)

*   **Alias**：**Last Colonizers · Iron Desperadoes · Exiles of Gaanan**

A carbon-based civilization from the Taren star system, whose origins and evolutionary path closely parallel humanity — yet they chose a far more radical course of self-modification: mass cyborgification, Dyson spheres, and ultimately the exhaustion of their homeworld Gaanan. With nowhere left to turn, they broadcast a distress signal into the cosmos. Only Earth responded — sparking a thirty-year war.

## I. Biology

*   **Origin**: Carbon-based, two-sex reproduction; evolutionary path mirrors humanity
*   **Build**: Comparable to a chimpanzee — no myostatin, high basal metabolism, exceptional natural muscle mass
*   **Four arms**: A natural trait, not a cybernetic modification
    *   **Upper arms (×2)**: Mounted at the shoulders; massive, primary for combat and heavy labor
    *   **Lower arms (×2)**: Mounted at the waist; smaller, used for fine manipulation
*   **Skin**: Predominantly grey tones; varies greatly with degree of cyborgification
*   **Head**: Hairless; deep facial features; eyes frequently modified with glowing cybernetic lenses

## II. Cyborgification

*   **Mainstream**: Voluntary mass cyborgification — partial, full-body, and electronic-brain variants
*   **Tactical backpack**: Standard-issue micro-circulation blood dialysis pump; delivers concentrated glucose, homeworld herbs (Rhodiola-equivalent), vitamins and minerals via body interface — countering the equivalent of high-altitude sickness on Earth
*   **Weapons**: Energy-based firearms, interfaced directly through cybernetic ports
*   **Atmosphere**: Taren require hyperoxic environments; prolonged Earth deployment requires circulation support. This environment is lethal to humans — coexistence is biologically impossible

## III. Civilization Arc

*   **Path**: Carbon-based → Cyborgification Age → Dyson Sphere Age → Resource Collapse → Distress Signal
*   **The Trap**: Energy consumption persistently outpaced cosmic replenishment — a mirror of humanity's potential future
*   **Civil War Outcome**: War faction seized power; peace faction leadership imprisoned (not executed); entire star system's resources committed to the jump — the fleet arrived shattered
*   **Stalemate Causes**: Civil war attrition + jump fuel exhaustion + need to preserve Earth's resources intact (no orbital bombardment) + ongoing military losses

## IV. Factions

*   **War Faction**: Dominant; goal is full occupation and terraforming Earth into a hyperoxic environment
*   **Peace Faction**: Leadership imprisoned; legacy preserved through the dimensional bracer — a civilization seed vault gifted to humanity
*   **Moderate Faction**: Advocates Earth-sharing with humans; rejected by both sides due to atmospheric incompatibility and human moral refusal
*   **Defectors**: A rare few Taren have sided with humanity, providing translation and technical aid

## V. Relations with Humanity

*   **Language**: Peace faction Taren have mastered human languages; full mutual translation possible. The Taren spoken tongue is called Velkor; their writing is a logographic ideographic system
*   **Technology Transfer**: Humanity gained high-density storage chips, compute chips, and neural-network chip technology during the war; spatial technology remains beyond reach due to a fundamental conceptual gap
*   **Legacy**: The dimensional bracer contains the peace faction's advice on sustainable development; partial energy weapon principles have been absorbed by humanity
*   **Biological Intersection**: Reproductive systems are analogous to humans; Taren military codes forbid contact — motivated by cross-species disease risk, not morality

## Appendix · Velkor Script

*   The Taren use a logographic writing system — each glyph encodes a semantic concept, not a sound.
*   Script family: **Style A Angular** (straight lines only, no curves), derived from ancestral four-arm pictographs.
*   Encoding: Unicode Private Use Area, U+E000 and above.
