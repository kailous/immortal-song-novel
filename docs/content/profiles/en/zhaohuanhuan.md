# Zhao Huanhuan

![Zhao Huanhuan](../../../images/characters/赵嬛嬛.webp)

*   **Alias**：**Song Dynasty Imperial Princess · Ladder to the Stolen Throne · Wielder of Imperial Authority**

An Imperial Princess of the Song Dynasty — once the most radiant jewel of the royal family, offered as a peace sacrifice by her own father and brothers in the Jingkang Humiliation. After seeing through the rot of the old order, she chose to betray her class, becoming Lu Chen's most ruthless political operator. Holding the imperial seal, she lays the legal groundwork for a new civilization.

## 0. Name & Identity Evolution

*   **Birth name**: **Zhao Huanhuan** (huán huán)
*   **Title**: Song Dynasty Imperial Princess (Empress Consort)
*   **Historical basis**: Composite of Róufu Imperial Consort / Maode Imperial Consort (real historical victims)
*   **Age**: 18 (at the outbreak of the Jingkang Catastrophe — offered as spoils of war at her most beautiful age)

## 1. Appearance: Imperial Glass & Tempered Blade

*   **Visual pressure**: Her face carries the deep imperial elegance of the Song royal lineage — **coldly and absolutely beautiful**, with every gesture radiating a refinement that makes common mortals feel ashamed of their inadequacy.
*   **Coldly beautiful**: Having seen through her father's and brothers' hypocrisy, her eyes now hold **bottomless cold fury and ruthlessness**. Though she appears as delicate as imperial glass in a gilded cage, the pressure she radiates when appraising power ministers is like a poison-tempered ice blade.
*   **Signature dress**: Strictly observing the highest Song imperial court protocol, she commonly wears the **deep violet (deep purple)** or **black** straight-collar crossed-lapel long robe representing the highest rank. Complex 'crinkled gold craft' embroidery of twin phoenixes through peonies takes on, on her body, the sharp quality of **metal chain-mail and thorn brambles**.

## 2. Background: Highest Jewel Sacrificed by Her State

*   **Class-betrayal awakening**: While being abandoned by the empire on a long road to a political marriage, she saw through the greed and hypocrisy of the Song civil-official clique and aristocratic families. She decided to completely betray the royal privileged class and transform into the old era's 'reckoner.'
*   **The humiliating 'peace marriage'**: As a political sacrifice under the 'prevention of military generals and consort families' clan law, after the nation's spine was trampled by enemy cavalry, she was bound by her cowardly father and brothers as war spoils onto a desperate cart heading north toward a nomadic empire.
*   **Steely salvation**: In the most brutal sandstorms at the border, she witnessed the protagonist lead iron cavalry and turn the enemy's welcoming procession to minced flesh. The protagonist's dimensionally-striking cold violent aesthetics showed her the only 'steel' capable of shattering humiliation.

## 3. Core Character: Extreme Cold Operator

*   **Treasonous interest alliance**: She proactively attaches herself to the protagonist — viewed as a crude warlord — and uses the legitimate imperial princess identity as his endorsement.
*   **Ruthless political mind**: Deep familiarity with the struggle rules between the Privy Council and the chancellery; skilled at helping the protagonist form alliances and schemes within the court through intrigue, sowing discord, and even assassination.
*   **Cold 'open conspiracy'**: She once personally orchestrated 'stealing the imperial seal' and releasing iron evidence of a new emperor 'patricide and usurpation,' forcing the Song to split into five false emperors — directly creating for the protagonist a perfect chaos where 'acting on heaven's mandate to pacify the rebellion' is justified.

## 4. Core Role: Ladder to the Stolen Throne & Dual-Core Complement

*   **Legal unlocker**: She is the protagonist group's ultimate legal ladder to the peak of Song power. The imperial seal in her hands is the protagonist's core weapon for an unassailable claim to the throne.
*   **Perfect synergy with the First Female Lead**:
    * **Merchant widow (Lu Xiaoxiao)**: Responsible for logistics, finance, and industrial coordination with absolute loyalty.
    * **Scheming princess (Zhao Huanhuan)**: Responsible for legal legitimacy, court infighting, intelligence systems, and grand strategic overview.
*   **Political grand steward**: Responsible for cultivating scholar-gentry clans and stabilizing the political ecology of the rear.
