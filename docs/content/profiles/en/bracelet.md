# Dimensional Bracelet

![Dimensional Bracelet](../../../images/次元手环.webp)

*   **Alias**：**Alien High-Dimensional Folded Space Artifact · Humanity's Last Bet**

An absolutely rigid artifact captured from aliens. Inside is folded an independent high-dimensional space — conservatively estimated at the volume of a 20-story office building. Temperature: absolute zero. Pressure: perfect vacuum. Time flow rate: zero. Humanity has sealed within it the crystallization of all its final technology: ultra-precision lathes, cold fusion batteries, supercomputing servers, and the digitized legacy of all human civilization.

## 1. Physical Form & Material

*   **Structural appearance**: No mechanical joint seams anywhere, presenting a deep matte metallic texture. The bracelet surface is covered in micro-engraved complex recursive patterns impossible to fully resolve with the naked eye — resembling a hybrid of ultra-high-density quantum integrated circuits and unknown alien totems. These cold, non-reflective 'silver-gray etched lines' are not ordinary decorative engravings, but high-dimensional data conduits directly etched at the molecular level — radiating an extreme cyberpunk and heavy industrial aesthetic.
*   **Absolute rigidity (strong-force material)**: The material resembles a dimensionally-reduced 'teardrop,' with atoms locked tight by strong nuclear force. It is an indestructible high-dimensional artifact, immune to all physical strikes, extreme heat (Unit 731 incinerator and beyond), and chemical corrosion — and successfully withstood the extreme gravitational tidal tearing of spacetime transit.

## 2. Unlocking Mechanism & Plot Lock

*   **No fantasy binding**: The bracelet has **no 'blood recognition,' 'brainwave binding,' or 'retinal identification'** gimmicks. As a high-dimensional minimalist artifact, it relies entirely on a physical 'touch-pad combination lock' to open.
*   **Hell-level difficulty**: The micro-engraved circuits on the bracelet surface itself constitute an enormously complex three-dimensional 'touch combination array.' With no conventional optical guidance or vibration error-correction feedback, the operator must trace and activate the correct geometric energy-transmission path among tens of thousands of complex patterns — relying on extremely refined fingertip micro-sensitivity, abnormal muscle memory, and millisecond-precise press rhythm.
*   **Spacetime sequelae (memory fragmentation)**: During the protagonist's crossing (quantum-state reconstitution), brain neural synapse recording severely lagged. Even knowing he carries a world-altering artifact, he has lost this astronomical 'unlock gesture memory' in the data bottleneck of the spacetime leap.
*   **Phased unsealing (key driving the tech tree)**: The bracelet code memory will not recover all at once — it surfaces gradually as the protagonist's hippocampus slowly heals or from extreme external stimulation (life-or-death situations, inspiration from specific Song Dynasty technological breakthroughs). This **phased code unlocking** perfectly controls the rhythm of 2056 technology's dimensional strike (e.g., early: only 'agricultural seed compartment' unlocked; mid-game: 'basic mother-machine compartment'; late-game: 'high-dimensional energy/weapons compartment').

## 3. Core Reserve Pool

The bracelet interior is an **absolutely vacuum, time-stopped, high-dimensional folded warehouse (cannot store living beings)**, carrying humanity's 2056 final trump cards for wandering or counterattacking:

1.  **Peak Computing LLM Library & Full Human Tech Tree**
    *   An extremely dense and powerful quantum computing terminal (informally: the offline 'think tank').
    *   **Anti-collapse limitation**: The LLM **absolutely does not provide 'one-click creation'**! It only provides engineering blueprints, chemical formulas, mechanical calculations, and error-correction paths. To build a steam engine or flintlock gun, the protagonist must still source raw materials in the Song Dynasty, find suitable iron and coal deposits, guide ancient craftsmen in 'primitive-method steel-making,' and endure the brutal industrial process of 'concept → craftsman R&D → failure → finalization.'
2.  **Industrial Seeds & Key Materials**
    *   Contains the highest-precision 5-axis/7-axis CNC machine tool prototype (the mother-of-all-mother-machines), special alloy material sample packs, and ultra-high-purity chemical catalysts. This bridges the most difficult step of leaping from 'manual smelting' directly to 'precision mechanical machining' in Song times.
3.  **Human Survival Hope (Agriculture & Medicine)**
    *   High-yield, cold/drought/flood-resistant genetically-modified super-crop seed library (future variants of potatoes, sweet potatoes, hybrid rice). This is the protagonist's absolute foundation for early disaster relief, winning over refugees, and establishing the basic strategic territory for the struggle for power.
    *   Basic high-efficiency antibiotic synthesis mother-cultures and structural formulas, for dimensionally striking Song Dynasty plagues and battlefield infections.
4.  **Micro Cold Fusion Reactor (Energy Crisis Foreshadowing)**
    *   **Energy conservation limitation**: The precise instruments and mother-machine operations inside the bracelet strongly depend on energy. The initial cold-fusion energy rods stored in the bracelet are extremely limited. Before establishing a Song Dynasty power grid or finding a primitive method for purifying nuclear fuel, every watt must be carefully calculated. If energy runs out, all modern equipment extracted from the bracelet becomes scrap metal.

## 4. Core Anti-Collapse Iron Rules Summary

1.  **Cannot function as a 'four-dimensional pocket'**: Items extracted are strictly limited by unlock permission and energy consumption; cannot instantly store enemies in combat or giant objects into the bracelet.
2.  **Must undergo digestion period**: No matter how advanced the blueprints and mother-machines extracted, they are still constrained by the Song Dynasty's 'basic materials science laws.' (E.g., the mother-machine can cut a gun barrel, but if Song craftsmen haven't smelted steel of sufficient strength for the barrel, it will still explode.)
3.  **Absolute dead-matter principle**: Vacuum field — no living beings can be stored.
