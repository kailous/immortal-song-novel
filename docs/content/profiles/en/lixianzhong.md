# Li Xianzhong

![Li Xianzhong](../../../images/characters/李显忠.webp)

*   **Alias**：**Returned General · Yan'an Li Clan · Lu Chen's Fierce Mirror**

Born Li Shibo, a 'returned' Song general. Carrying deep vengeance for his family and nation, he fought his way back from Jin territory to the Song. Bold and unconventional, he is the most steadfast supporter of Lu Chen's asymmetric warfare — and the protagonist's fierce, kindred mirror image within the military system.

## 0. Role (Anchor)

- **Identity**: [Historical figure] Born Li Shibo, Southern Song 'returned' general (descendant of the Yan'an Li clan).
- **Relationship**: Lu Chen's combat mentor and kindred spirit against oppression.
- **Narrative function**: As a frontier general who fought back from Jin territory, he represents the radical faction of 'recovering lost lands at any cost,' and is Lu Chen's most steadfast supporter of asymmetric warfare.

## 1. Physical Profile

- **Physique**: Full of powerful energy, but bearing obvious old wound marks (arrow wounds, fall-from-horse wounds).
- **Expression**: Sharp eyes, often carrying an indomitable killing spirit, beard slightly unkempt from years of campaigning.
- **Equipment**: Western-style heavy armor, skilled with a long battle-axe or broad sword (designed from his background as a Jin military officer).

## 2. Behavior & Combat Style

- **Unconventional**: He doesn't venerate official formation tactics — more devoted to real-combat survival skills forged at the brink of death.
- **Suspicion & trust**: Due to his 'returned person' (defector) identity, he is naturally skeptical of the Song court — which makes it easier for him to form a 'lone minister, ill-starred son' type of trust with the mysterious Lu Chen.

## 3. Core Character & Conflict

- **Family and national vengeance**: His family being exterminated by the Jin is his sole driving force.
- **Outsider identity**: Marginalized among southern generals — this 'outsider' loneliness is the bond connecting him with Lu Chen.

## 4. Key Assets & Faction Relations

- **Returned-person forces**: His troops are mostly iron-blooded warriors who escaped back from the north — this force's combat style is the most compatible with Lu Chen's extreme lethality.

## 5. Story Log

- **Initial arrival at Lin'an**: Around this node or during border patrol, first encounters the wandering Lu Chen.
- **Connoisseur**: He is the only general who, at a glance, recognizes that the people Lu Chen killed (Wang San etc.) died from 'high-efficiency body-dismantling' rather than ordinary fighting.

## Appendix: Historical Research Notes

- **[Think Tank Research]**: Li Xianzhong is one of the most legendary generals of the early Southern Song. His entire family died at Jin hands, and he led followers back south. This 'backs-to-the-wall' spirit fits the hard sci-fi tone very well.
- **[Writer Team Notes]**: He can serve as a 'wild man' mirror of Lu Chen — on certain insane combat plans, he will be Lu Chen's most steadfast ally.
